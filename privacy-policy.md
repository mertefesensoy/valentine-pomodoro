Privacy Policy

Valentine Pomodoro does not collect, store, transmit, or share personal data.

Data Collection

No account is required.

No analytics, tracking, advertising, or third-party SDKs are used to collect user data.

On-Device Storage
App data such as timer settings, session stats, and love notes are stored locally on your device using on-device storage. This data is not sent to any server.

Notifications
If you enable notifications, the app requests permission to send local notifications for timer completion. Notification permissions can be changed anytime in iOS Settings.
